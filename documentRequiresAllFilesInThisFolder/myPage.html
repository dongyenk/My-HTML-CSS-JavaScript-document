Some Of Those Traditional Roguelikes:

Brogue

  • Get it here
  • Easy to start playing
  • Intuitive and simple UI
  • No complicated race and class system
  • Character builds determined by what is randomly found in dungeons
  • Very nice ASCII graphics (keyboard symbols)

Stone Soup

  • Play it here
  • Harder to start playing
  • But has a thorough tutorial
  • Has many races, classes and gods to choose
  • Active Reddit and Discord communities
  • Has clan based tournaments
  • Pixel art and ASCII graphics available

Nethack

  • Get it here
  • One of the older roguelikes
  • Cryptic; a lot of the game's systems aren't obvious
  • Requires extreme trial and error or manual/online-guide reading
  • Charming, creative and full of lots of interesting reading
  • Also has clan based tournaments
